**1.** (习题 6-25)

&emsp;&emsp;$1\to2$, 考虑可逆等温膨胀过程,
$$
\Delta S_{1\to 2}=\int_1^2\frac{\delta Q}{T}=\frac{1}{T_1}\int_1^2\d A=\frac{1}{T_1}\int_1^2 \frac{nRT_1\d V}{V}=nR\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1},\\
\Delta S_{1\to2}\approx 5.76\u{J/K}.
$$
&emsp;&emsp;$1\to3\to2$, 氢气有 $i=5$; 分别考虑可逆等压膨胀和可逆等体膨胀,
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\Delta S_{1\to3} &= \int_1^3\frac{\delta Q}{T}\\
	&= \int_1^3\frac{\d E}{T}+\int_1^3\frac{p\d V}{T}\\
	&= \int_1^3\frac{\frac{5}{2}nR\d V}{V}+\int_1^3\frac{nR\d V}{V}\\
	&= \frac{7}{2}nR\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1};
}
$$
此时温度满足 $\frac{T_3}{T_1}=\frac{V_3}{V_1}=\frac{V_2}{V_1}$.
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\Delta S_{3\to 2} &= \int_3^2\frac{\d E}{T}\\
	&= \int_3^2\frac{\frac{5}{2}nR\d T}{T}\\
	&= \frac{5}{2}nR\ln\frac{T_2}{T_3}\\
	&= -\frac{5}{2}nR\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1}.
}
$$
因此 $\Delta S_{1\to3\to2}=nR\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1}=\Delta S_{1\to 2}$.

&emsp;&emsp;$1\to4\to2$, 氢气有 $\gamma=\frac{7}{5}$; 前者绝热无熵变, 过程前后满足 $p_1V_1^\gamma=p_2V_4^\gamma$, 因此
$$
\ln\frac{V_4}{V_1}=\frac{1}{\gamma}\ln\frac{p_1}{p_2}=\frac{1}{\gamma}\ln\frac{V_1}{V_2}\Ra\ln\frac{V_2}{V_4}=-\ln\frac{V_4}{V_2}=-\br{\ln\frac{V_4}{V_1}+\ln 2}=\frac{2}{7}\ln 2.
$$
后者仍考虑可逆等压膨胀, 形如上述 $\Delta S_{1\to 3}$ 可知
$$
\Delta S_{4\to 2}=nR\ln\frac{V_2}{V_1}=\Delta S_{1\to 2}.
$$
&emsp;&emsp;结论: $S$ 是状态量, 与平衡态间转移的过程无关.

**2.** (习题 6-27)

&emsp;&emsp;已知常数有
$$
C_{\u{water},p}=4.22\x10^3\u{J/(kg\cdot K)},\quad C_{\u{ice},p}=2.09\x10^3\u{J/(kg\cdot K)},\\
\quad \lambda_{\u{ice}}=3.34\x10^5\u{J/kg}.
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(1) (若只计算水的熵变:)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\Delta S=\int_{1}^{3}\frac{\delta Q}{T} &= \int_{-10\u{\degree C}}^{0\u{\degree C}}\frac{mC_{\u{ice},p}\d T}{T}+\frac{m\lambda_{\u{ice}}}{T_0}+\int_{0\u{\degree C}}^{10\u{\degree C}}\frac{mC_{\u{water},p}\d T}{T}\\
	&= mC_{\u{ice},p}\ln\frac{273}{263}+\frac{m\lambda_{\u{ice}}}{273\u{K}}+mC_{\u{water},p}\ln\frac{283}{273}\\
	&\approx 1453\u{J/K}.
}
$$
(若假设环境恒温 $10\cent$, 则还需要加上环境熵变:)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\Delta S_{\u{env}}=\int_{1}^{3}\frac{\delta Q}{T} &= \frac{-mC_{\u{ice,p}}\Delta T}{T_{\u{env}}}+\frac{-m\lambda_{\u{ice}}}{T_{\u{env}}}+\frac{-mC_{\u{water},p}\Delta T}{T_{\u{env}}}\\
	&= -1403\u{J/K},
}
$$

$$
\Delta S_{\u{total}}=\Delta S+\Delta S_{\u{env}}\approx50\u{J/K}.
$$

&emsp;&emsp;(2) 冰变为水前后, 水的熵变为
$$
\Delta S_0=\frac{m\lambda_{\u{ice}}}{273\u{K}}\approx1223\u{J/K}.
$$
根据 Boltzmann 熵的定义,
$$
\ln\frac{W_{\u{water}}}{W_\u{ice}}=\frac{\Delta S_0}{k}.
$$
可知
$$
\frac{W_{\u{water}}}{W_{\u{ice}}}=\exp(8.86\x10^{25}).
$$

**3.** (熵的概念理解)

&emsp;&emsp;熵是系统微观状态数的度量, 熵越大, 系统所对应的微观状态数越多 (常说的 "更无序"). 它也在宏观层面描述了热量交换与温度的关系.
