**1.** (习题七 38)

&emsp;&emsp;(1) 二者交点 $(0,0),(1,1)$, 则
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= \abs{\int_0^1 y\d y^2-\int_0^1 x^2\d x}\\
	&= \frac{1}{3}.
}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(2) 从 $x_0=0$ 到 $x_1=\frac{\pi}{4}$,
$$
S_1=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}(\cos x-\sin x)\d x=\sqrt 2-1.
$$
从 $x_1=\frac{\pi}{4}$ 到 $x_2=\frac{5\pi}{4}$,
$$
S_2=\int_{\frac{\pi}{4}}^{\frac{5\pi}{4}}(\sin x-\cos x)\d x=2\sqrt 2.
$$
从 $x_2=\frac{5\pi}{4}$ 到 $x_3=2\pi$,
$$
S_3=\int_{\frac{5\pi}{4}}^{2\pi}(\cos x-\sin x)\d x=1+\sqrt 2.
$$
则
$$
S=S_1+S_2+S_3=4\sqrt 2.
$$

**2.** (习题七 40)

&emsp;&emsp;(1)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= \frac{1}{2}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}(x'y-y'x)\d t}\\
	&= \frac{a^2}{2}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}((1-\cos t)^2-\sin t(t-\sin t))\d t}\\
	&= \frac{a^2}{2}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}(2-t\sin t-2\cos t)\d t}\\
	&= \frac{a^2}{2}\abs{4\pi+2\pi-0}\\
	&= 3\pi a^2.
}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(3)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= \frac{1}{2}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}(x'y-y'x)\d t}\\
	&= \frac{1}{2}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}\br{-\frac{3c^4}{ab}\cos^2 t\sin^4 t-\frac{3c^4}{ab}\sin^2 t\cos^4 t}\d t}\\
	&= \frac{3c^4}{2|ab|}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}\sin^2 t\cos^2 t\d t}\\
	&= \frac{3c^4}{2|ab|}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}2t(\sin t\cos^3 t-\cos t\sin^ 3t)\d t}\\
	&= \frac{3c^4}{4|ab|}\abs{\int_0^{2\pi}t\sin 4t\cdot\d t}\\
	&= \frac{3c^4}{8|ab|}.
}
$$

**3.** (习题七 42)

&emsp;&emsp;(2) 令 $x=r\cos t,~y=r\sin t$, 则原方程给出
$$
r^2(\cos^4 t+\sin^4 t)=1\Ra r^2=\frac{1}{\cos^4 t+\sin^4 t}\quad (t\in[0,2\pi]).
$$
因此
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= \frac{1}{2}\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\d t}{\cos^4 t+\sin^4 t}\\
	&= \frac{1}{2}\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\d t}{1-\frac{1}{2}\sin^2 2t}\\
	&= 4\int_0^\pi\frac{\d t}{3-\cos t}.
}
$$
其中
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\int \frac{\d t}{3-\cos t} &= \int\frac{\frac{2}{1+u^2}}{3-\frac{1-u^2}{1+u^2}}\d u\\
	&= \int\frac{\d u}{1+2u^2}\\
	&= \frac{1}{\sqrt 2}\int\frac{\d(\sqrt 2 u)}{1+(\sqrt 2u)^2}\\
	&= \frac{\arctan\br{\sqrt 2\tan\frac{t}{2}}}{\sqrt 2}+C.
}
$$
则
$$
S=\sqrt 2\pi.
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(4) 令 $x=r\cos t,~y=r\sin t$, 则原方程给出
$$
r^2=\sin 2t\quad (t\in[0,\frac{\pi}{2}]\cup[\pi,\frac{3\pi}{2}]).
$$
所以
$$
S=2\cdot\frac{1}{2}\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin 2t\d t=1.
$$

**4.** (习题七 43)

**5.** (习题七 46)

&emsp;&emsp;固定 $z\in[0,1]$, 考虑 $S(\Omega_z)$, 初等几何分析可知, 若令 $\theta=\arcsin\sqrt{1-z^2}=\arccos z$, 则
$$
2S(\Omega_z)=\theta+(1-z^2)(\pi-2\theta)-\frac{1}{4}\sin 2\theta=\theta+(1-z^2)(\pi-2\theta)-z\sqrt{1-z^2}.
$$
 研究各项不定积分:
$$
\int\arccos z\cdot\d z=z\arccos z-\sqrt{1-z^2}+C.
$$

$$
\int z\sqrt{1-z^2}\d z=\frac{1}{3}(1-z^2)^{3/2}+C.
$$

$$
\int z^2\arccos z\cdot\d z=\frac{z^3}{3}\arccos z+\frac{1}{6}\int\frac{z^2\d z^2}{\sqrt{1-z^2}}=\frac{z^3}{3}\arccos z-\frac{1}{9}(1-z^2)^{3/2}+\frac{1}{3}\sqrt{1-z^2}+C.
$$

代入下式即可.
$$
\env{aligned}{
	V &= \int_0^1 2S(\Omega_z)\d z\\
	&= -1+\frac{2\pi}{3}+\frac{4}{9}-\frac{1}{3}\\
	&= \frac{2\pi}{3}-\frac{8}{9}.
}
$$
> *Correction.*
>
> &emsp;&emsp;听说用球坐标算方便一点. 回忆一下球坐标的 $(r,\theta,\varphi)$, 体积元是 $r^2\sin\theta\cdot\d r\cdot\d\theta\cdot\d\varphi$ (注意三个切向恰好是正交的, 类似于二维下所谓的自然坐标架).

**6.** (习题七 48)

&emsp;&emsp;先考察对于极坐标上 $(0,0),(a,\alpha),(b,\beta)~(a,b>0,~0\le \alpha<\beta\le \pi)$ 三点构成三角形绕极轴旋转的立体的体积. 初等几何分析得:
$$
\env{aligned}{
	V_0 ={}&\frac{\pi}{3}b^3\sin^2\beta\cos\beta\\
	&+\frac{\pi}{3}(b^2\sin^2\beta+a^2\sin^2\alpha+ab\sin\beta\sin\alpha)(a\cos\alpha-b\cos\beta)\\
	&-\frac{\pi}{3}a^3\sin^2\alpha\cos\alpha\\
	={}&\frac{\pi}{3}a^2b\sin\alpha\sin(\beta-\alpha)+\frac{\pi}{3}ab^2\sin\beta\sin(\beta-\alpha)\\
	={}&\frac{\pi}{3}ab\sin(\beta-\alpha)(a\sin\alpha+b\sin\beta).
}
$$
若 $\beta=\alpha+\Delta\theta$, $\Delta\theta>0$ 极小, 由于已有 $\sin(\beta-\alpha)=\Delta\theta+o(\Delta\theta)$, 其他因式的一阶小量可以忽略, 则令 $a=b=r$, 有
$$
V_0=\frac{2\pi}{3}r^3\sin\theta\cdot\Delta\theta.
$$
因此, 对题目所求立体, 直接对 $\theta$ 积分, 有
$$
\env{aligned}{
	V &= \int_\alpha^\beta\frac{\pi}{3}2r^3\sin\theta\d\theta=\frac{2\pi}{3}\int_\alpha^\beta r^3\sin\theta\cdot\d\theta.
}
$$
> *Correction.*
>
> &emsp;&emsp;或者, 面积微元是 $r\cdot\d r\cdot\d\theta$, 重积分
> $$
> \int_\alpha^\beta\d\theta\int_0^{r(\theta)}r\d r\cdot 2\pi r\sin\theta
> $$
> 也能 (方便地?) 得到结果.

**7.** (习题七 49)

&emsp;&emsp;(2)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	L &= \int_0^{2\pi}\sqrt{(a\theta)^2+a^2}\d\theta\\
	&= |a|\int_0^{2\pi}\sqrt{1+\theta^2}\d\theta.
}
$$
回忆有
$$
\env{aligned}{
	\int\sqrt{1+x^2}\d x &= x\sqrt{x^2+1}-\int\frac{(x^2+1)-1}{\sqrt{1+x^2}}\d x\\
	&= x\sqrt{x^2+1}-\int\sqrt{x^2+1}\d x+\int\frac{\d x}{\sqrt{1+x^2}}\\
	&= \frac{1}{2}x\sqrt{x^2+1}+\frac{1}{2}\int\frac{\d\sinh u}{\cosh u}\quad (x=\sinh u)\\
	&= \frac{1}{2}x\sqrt{x^2+1}+\frac{1}{2}u+C\\
	&= \frac{1}{2}x\sqrt{x^2+1}+\frac{1}{2}\ln(x+\sqrt{1+x^2})+C.
}
$$

所以
$$
L=\frac{|a|}{2}\br{2\pi\sqrt{1+4\pi^2}+\ln(2\pi+\sqrt{1+4\pi^2})}.
$$
