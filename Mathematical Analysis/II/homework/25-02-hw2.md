**1.** (习题七 51)

&emsp;&emsp;(1)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= 2\pi\int_0^1 \sqrt{2px}\sqrt{1+\br{\frac{p}{\sqrt{2px}}}^2}\d x\\
	&= 2\pi\int_0^1\sqrt{2px+p^2}\d x\\
	&= \l.\frac{2\pi}{3p}\br{2px+p^2}^{\frac{3}{2}}\r|_0^1\\
	&= \frac{2\pi}{3p}\br{(2p+p^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}-p^3}.
}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(3) 方程给出 $x=a\cos^3 t,~y=a\sin^3 t,~t\in[0,2\pi]$, 根据对称性, 有:
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= 2\pi\int_0^\pi y\sqrt{x'(t)^2+y'(t)^2}\d t\\
	&= 2\pi \int_0^\pi a\sin^3 t\sqrt{(-3a\cos^2 t\sin t)^2+(3a\sin^2 t\cos t)^2}\d t\\
	&= 6\pi a^2\int_0^\pi\sin^3 t|\cos t\sin t|\d t\\
	&= 12\pi a^2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^4 t\cdot\d \sin t\\
	&= \frac{12}{5}\pi a^2.
}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;(5)
$$
\env{aligned}{
	S &= 2\pi\int_0^{2\pi}a^2(1-\cos t)\sqrt{(1-\cos t)^2+(\sin t)^2}\d t\\
	&= 2\pi a^2\int_0^{2\pi}(1-\cos t)\sqrt{2-2\cos t}\d t\\
	&= 4\sqrt 2\pi a^2\l.\br{-\frac{2}{3}\sqrt{2-u}(u+4)}\r|_0^2\\
	&= \frac{64}{3}\pi a^2.
}
$$

**3.** (习题七 54)

&emsp;&emsp;由 Guldin 第一定理, 设质心坐标 $(0,\ol y)$, 则
$$
2\pi\ol y\cdot\pi R=4\pi R^2\Ra \ol y=\frac{2}{\pi}R.
$$
因此质心坐标 $\br{0,\frac{2}{\pi}R}$.

&emsp;&emsp;令 $x=R\cos t,~y=R\sin t,~t\in[0,\pi]$, 关于 $x$ 轴的转动惯量即
$$
J_x= \int_0^\pi R^3\sin^2 t\d t= \frac{\pi}{2}R^3.
$$
由数学上或者物理上的对称性,
$$
J_y=J_x=\frac{\pi}{2}R^3.
$$

**4.** (习题七 56)

&emsp;&emsp;不妨以 $\Gamma$ 为 $x$ 轴, 设 $\Gamma_{AB}:x=x(t),~y=y(t),~z=z(t),~t\in[0,1]$, 由于 $|\Gamma_{AB}|=1$ 且为线段, 可以令
$$
\A t\in(0,1),~x'(t)^2+y'(t)^2+z'(t)^2=:k_x^2+k_y^2+k_z^2=1.
$$
则
$$
\env{aligned}{
	J&=\int_0^1(y(t)^2+z(t)^2)\sqrt{x'(t)^2+y'(t)^2+z'(t)^2}\d t\\
	&=\int_0^1(y(t)^2+z(t)^2)\d t\\
	&=\int_0^1\br{k_y^2t^2+2k_yb_yt+k_z^2t^2+2k_zb_zt+b_y^2+b_z^2}\d t\\
	&= \frac{1}{3}(k_y^2+k_z^2)+(k_yb_y+k_zb_z)+(b_y^2+b_z^2).
}
$$
同时已知
$$
b_y^2+b_z^2=a^2,\quad (k_y+b_y)^2+(k_z+b_z)^2=b^2.
$$
所以
$$
J=\frac{1}{3}(a^2+ab+b^2).
$$
