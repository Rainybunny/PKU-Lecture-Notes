$$
\begin{array}{r|l}
	1 & i\gets 2\\
	2 & j\gets 2\\
	3 & \textbf{while}~~j\le n~~\textbf{do}\\
	4 & \qquad \textbf{if}~~a_{j-1}\neq a_j~~\textbf{then}\\
	5 & \qquad \qquad a_i\gets a_j\\
	6 & \qquad \qquad i\gets i+1 \\
	7 & \qquad j\gets j+1\\
	8 & m \gets i-1 \qquad \texttt{// final length}
\end{array}
$$

**3.**
$$
\begin{array}{r|l}
	1 & \textbf{function}~~\text{findCircle}(L)\to\text{Node}\mid\text{Nothing}:\\
	2 & \qquad s \gets \textit{head}\\
	3 & \qquad t \gets \textit{head}\\
	4 & \qquad \textbf{while}~~t\neq\textit{nullptr} \land s\neq t~~\textbf{do}\\
	5 & \qquad \qquad t\gets\text{next}(L,t)\\
	6 & \qquad \qquad \textbf{if}~~t=\textit{nullptr}\lor s=t~~\textbf{then}\\
	7 & \qquad \qquad \qquad \textbf{break}\\
	8 & \qquad \qquad s \gets \text{next}(L,s)\\
	9 & \qquad \qquad t \gets \text{next}(L,t)\\
	10& \qquad \textbf{if}~~t=\textit{nullptr}~~\textbf{then}\\
	11& \qquad \qquad \textbf{return}~~\text{Nothing}\\
	\\
	12& \qquad w\gets \textit{head}\\
	13& \qquad \textbf{repeat}\\
	14& \qquad \qquad w\gets\text{next}(L,w)\\
	15& \qquad \qquad t\gets\text{next}(L,t)\\
	16& \qquad \textbf{until}~~s=t\\
	\\
	17& \qquad s\gets\textit{head}\\
	18& \qquad \textbf{while}~~s\neq w~~\textbf{do}\\
	19& \qquad \qquad s\gets\text{next}(L,s)\\
	20& \qquad \qquad w\gets\text{next}(L,w)\\
	21& \qquad \textbf{return}~~s
\end{array}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;第 $12$ 行时 $s=t$ 是环上某个结点, 第 $17$ 行时 $w$ 领先 $s$ 了 $c$ 步, $c$ 是环的大小. $\mathcal O(1)$ 额外空间, $\mathcal O(n)$ 时间, $n$ 为链表结点数.
