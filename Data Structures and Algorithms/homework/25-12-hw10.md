**3.**

&emsp;&emsp;考虑先对 $S_2$ 中的元素$\bmod p$ 的结果, 使得 $a\neq b\Ra a\not\equiv b\pmod p$, 而 $x+y=\textit{target}\Ra x+y\equiv \textit{target}\pmod p$, 可以枚举 $S_1$ 中的元素快速查询结果. 对随机选取的 $p=k\log^2 N+\mathcal O(1)$, union bound 给出直接$\bmod p$ 映射的碰撞概率不超过 $\frac{1}{k}$, 不妨取定 $k=2$. 算法如下:
$$
\begin{array}{r|ll}
	1& \textbf{function}~~\opn{build}(S):\\
	2& \qquad \textbf{repeat} \qquad \t{\#}~\mathcal O(1)~\t{times}\\
	3& \qquad \qquad p\gets \text{randomly picked}~(k\log^2 N+\mathcal O(1))~\text{prime}\\
	4& \qquad \qquad {table}\gets\{i\mapsto S_i\bmod p:i\in [|S|]\}\\
	5& \qquad \textbf{until}~~\text{no collision occurred}\\
	6& \qquad \textbf{return}~~(p,\textit{table})\\\\
	7& \textbf{function}~~\opn{solve}(S_1,S_2,\textit{target}):\\
	8& \qquad \textit{res}\gets[]\\
	9& \qquad p,\textit{table}\gets\opn{build}(S_2)\\
	10& \qquad \textbf{for}~~i=1~~\textbf{to}~~|S_1|~~\textbf{do}\\
	11& \qquad \qquad \textbf{for}~~j\in\textit{table}[-S_{1,i}\bmod p]~~\textbf{do}\\
	12& \qquad \qquad \qquad \textbf{if}~~S_{1,i}+S_{2,j}\neq \textit{target}~~\textbf{then}\\
	13& \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \textbf{break}\qquad \t{\# all values are same}\\
	14& \qquad \qquad \qquad \textit{res}.\opn{append}((i,j))\\
	15& \qquad \textbf{return}~~\textit{res}
\end{array}
$$
&emsp;&emsp;期望复杂度 $\mathcal O(n)$.
